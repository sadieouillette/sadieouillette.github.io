Question #1

Nancy Jean Burkholder, a trans woman who was expelled from the 1991 Michigan Womyn’s Music Festival on account of violating the “womyn-born womyn” only policy, showed the festival organizers an ID (to prove her sex was listed as female) from which New England US State?

New Hampshire

Question #2

This legendary ballroom house featured in the 1990 film Paris is Burning has the same name as a type of warrior.

House of Ninja

Question #3

Sylvia Rivera and Marsha P. Johnson — two prominent trans activists who are widely known for being essential in the Stonewall Riots — met on which holiday that rhymes with a royal title (e.g. princess, king, etc.)?

Halloween (rhymes with queen)
